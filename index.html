Welcome to the Arduino side of 雪他 (yukita).

All of our codding from prototype one to our final product can be found here.

Hardware

Specific hardware requirements for each individual sketch is located withing the sketch it's self, but here are all the things we used from alpha to final release:

  • Arduino Uno
  • Relay Shield
  • Ethernet Shield

Software

We used a few different software platforms and Arduino libraries for this project:

  • RobotC
  • TouchOSC (iOS)
  • TouchOSC Editor (PC)
  • Blynk (iOS)
  • OSCuino (OSC Library)
